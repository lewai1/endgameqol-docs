# Introduction

Everything EndgameQoL adds to your Hytale server.

## Content Overview

EndgameQoL is a comprehensive endgame content plugin that extends Hytale with new weapons, armor, bosses, dungeons, NPCs, and progression systems. The table below summarizes everything included.

| Category | Count | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Weapons | 40 | Swords, daggers, longswords, spears, staffs, bows, shields, battleaxes, mace |
| Armor | 5 sets | Diving Crude, Adamantite, Mithril, Onyxium, Prisma |
| Bosses | 3 | Dragon Frost (1400 HP), Hedera (1800 HP), Golem Void (3500 HP) |
| Elites | 6 | Dragon Fire, Alpha Rex, Swamp Crocodile, Zombie Aberrant, Bramble Elite, Spectre Void |
| Dungeons | 2 | Frozen Dungeon, Swamp Dungeon |
| Tools | 8 | Pickaxes, hatchets, shovels |
| Accessories | 6 | Frostwalkers, Ocean Striders, Void Amulet, Blazefist, Pocket Garden, Hedera Seed |
| Traders | 3 | Vorthak, Korvyn, Morghul |
| Systems | 5 active | Combo Meter, Bounty Board, Achievements, Bestiary, Warden Trials |
| Languages | 5 | EN, FR, ES, PT-BR, RU |
| Database | Optional | SQLite, MySQL, MariaDB, PostgreSQL |

## Material Progression

EndgameQoL introduces four tiers of endgame materials, each stronger than the last. Progress through them by exploring increasingly dangerous content.

**Adamantite** (Tier 1 — Entry) → **Mithril** (Tier 2 — Intermediate) → **Onyxium** (Tier 3 — Advanced) → **Prisma** (Tier 4 — Endgame)

## Getting Started

Follow these four steps to begin your endgame journey.

1. **Install the Plugin** — Download from CurseForge and drop the JAR into your server's `Mods/` folder. See the [Installation Guide](installation) for full details.
2. **Gear Up** — Gather Adamantite ore and craft your first endgame armor and weapons at a crafting bench. Check traders like Vorthak for rare materials.
3. **Explore Dungeons** — Venture into the Frozen Dungeon or the Swamp Dungeon. Each contains unique enemies, loot chests, and a final boss encounter.
4. **Master the Endgame** — Complete bounties, unlock achievements, fill the bestiary, push through Warden Trials, and work your way up to Prisma-tier gear. Chain combos for maximum damage output.
