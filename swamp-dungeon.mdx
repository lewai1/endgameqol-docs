## Hedera Key Recipe

The **Hedera Key** is crafted exclusively at the **Hedera Autel**, a special bench found in the swamp biome. Gather all five materials before approaching.

| Material         | Qty | Source                      |
|------------------|:---:|:----------------------------|
| Hedera's Bramble |  1  | Bramble Elite drop          |
| Swamp Gem        |  1  | Swamp biome mining          |
| Swamp Ingot      |  1  | Smelted from Swamp Ore      |
| Crocodile Scale  |  1  | Swamp Crocodile drop        |
| Infused Rope     |  1  | Crafted (Rope + Swamp Gem)  |

## Boss — Hedera

| Stat           | Value                                                                                  |
|----------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Health         | **1800 HP**                                                                            |
| Phases         | 3 phases (Poison / Roots / Scream)                                                     |
| Player Scaling | 50% per additional player                                                              |
| Attacks        | Poison clouds, root entanglement, scream knockback                                     |
| Drops          | **Hedera Gem** + **Onyxium Bars** (3-7) + **Forest Essence** (10-15) + **Voidheart**  |

Bring antidotes for the poison phases. The root phase locks you in place — dodge the visual telegraph before roots spawn.

[View full boss guide](bosses)

## Chest Loot Tiers

### Common

Potions, arrows, torches, food. Found in early rooms near the dungeon entrance.

### Rare

Swamp Ingots, Swamp Gems, Onyxium Ore. Found in mid-dungeon combat rooms.

### Epic

Onyxium Bars, rare materials, enchanted gear. Found in late-dungeon locked rooms.

### Hidden

Secret chests behind breakable walls and underwater passages. Unique loot and bonus Swamp Currency.

> [!WARNING]
> **Block Protection:** Building is restricted inside the dungeon instance. Players cannot break or place blocks, preventing sequence-breaking. Toggle: `EnableDungeonBlockProtection`
