# <gradient data="#ff6600:#55ffff:#d16eff">Materials Guide</gradient>

This page lists all endgame materials added or used by Endgame & QoL and where to find them.

---

## <#ff6600>Hides & Leather</#ff6600>

### Storm Hide

A key crafting material used in most Mithril and Onyxium recipes (via Storm Leather).

| Source | Quantity |
|:-------|:---------|
| Dragon Frost | 3-6 (40%) |
| Yeti (vanilla) | 1-3 |
| Werewolf | 1 |
| Onyxium Encounter | 2-3 |
| Zone 4 Sewers chests | 1-3 |
| Frozen Dungeon towers | 1-3 |
| Frozen Dungeon hidden chests | 1-4 |
| Frozen Dungeon Dragon Zone | 3-5 |
| Warden Trial I/II/III/IV | 2-4 / 3-5 / 4-7 / 5-8 |

**Craftable** — 4 Cindercloth Scrap + 3 Heavy Leather + 3 Shadoweave Scrap + 3 Sturdy Chitin (Endgame Bench Tier 3)

---

### Prismic Hide

The highest-tier leather, required for Prisma armor.

**Craft only** — 4 Storm Leather + 3 Heavy Leather + 3 Shadoweave Scrap + 3 Ice Essence (Endgame Bench Tier 4)

! Storm Leather is tanned from Storm Hide at the Tannery.

---

### Apex Sovereign Leather

Tough leather from the Alpha Rex.

**Mob drops:**
- Alpha Rex — 2-3

---

### Sturdy Chitin

Tough exoskeleton plates from Scarak fighters. Used in Storm Hide crafting.

**Mob drops:**
- Scarak Fighter — 1-2

---

## <#55ffff>Bars & Ores</#55ffff>

### Prisma Bar

The rarest crafting bar, required for all Prisma-tier equipment.

**Mob drops:**
- Golem Void — 3-7 (guaranteed)

**Warden's Trial rewards:**
- Tier III: 1-3 | Tier IV: 3-6

**Craftable** — 1 Mithril Bar + 1 Onyxium Bar + 1 Emerald (Endgame Bench Tier 5)

! You can also trade Prisma Bars at the Vorthak shop.

---

## <#ff5555>Boss Materials</#ff5555>

### Dragon Heart

Unique trophy from the frost dragon. Used to upgrade the Endgame Bench.

**Mob drops:**
- Dragon Frost — 1 (guaranteed)

---

### Hedera Gem

Unique trophy from the forest guardian.

**Mob drops:**
- Hedera — 1 (guaranteed)

---

### Forest Essence

Concentrated natural energy. Used in Hedera weapon and portal crafting.

**Mob drops:**
- Hedera — 10-15 (guaranteed)
- Spirit Root — 1-3

---

## <#55ff55>Swamp Dungeon Materials</#55ff55>

### Swamp Ingot

Crafting material traded from Morghul or found in dungeon chests. Used to craft the Hedera Key.

**Source:** Morghul trader (6 Swamp Currency), dungeon chests

---

### Swamp Gem

A glowing green gem mined from breakable nodes inside the Swamp Dungeon. Used to craft the Hedera Key.

**Source:** Breakable gem nodes in the Swamp Dungeon

---

### Swamp Crocodile Scale

Tough scales from the Swamp Crocodile elite. Used to craft the Hedera Key.

**Mob drops:**
- Swamp Crocodile — 1 (guaranteed)

---

### Hedera's Bramble

A thorned trophy from the Bramble Elite. Used to craft the Hedera Key.

**Mob drops:**
- Bramble Elite — 1 (guaranteed)

---

### Infused Rope

Enchanted rope found in hidden chests deep in the Swamp Dungeon. Used to craft the Hedera Key.

**Source:** Hidden dungeon chests

---
